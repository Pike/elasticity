Elasticity in L10n Automation

The L10n automation requires quite a bit of elasticity. This graph shows the number of automation requests per hour as well as how much compute time that is. The time is estimated based on automation jobs taking somewhat around 10 seconds.

The numbers show little need for compute power in general, with short spikes of load for en-US landings triggering some 30mins of compute time.

The notable spike on June 29th is merge day, which recomputes most, and weighs in with some almost 3 hours of compute time.
